Biography

I am a researcher and lecturer at the Smart Mechatronics and Robotics Research Group in Saxion University of Applied Sciences with prof. Abeje Mersha , and a PhD candidate at the Robotics and Mechatronics Lab at the University of Twente , with dr. Chiara Gabellieri and prof. Antonio Franchi . My research focuses on the modelling and control of Aerial Robots for Physical Interaction tasks.
